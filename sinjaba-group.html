Sinjaba Group started as a real estate development company owned by the Sinjaba family, founded in 1990 by the late father Mohammad Reda Sinjaba.

 

Their scope of work expanded to include other businesses related to the field, such as Hospitality & other related projects.

Operated by the head office in Beirut, Sinjaba own a portfolio in Lebanon, Syria, Jordan, Dubai, and Egypt.

A Fresh Overhaul.

Sinjaba Group has been in the market for over 30 years, and with the young generations joining their team, they wanted to give Sinjaba Group a fresh look and feel to show that they are still on top of the game, focusing on the human experience. Keeping in mind the authentic Sinjaba feel.
